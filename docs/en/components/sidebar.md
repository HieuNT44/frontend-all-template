A Sidebar component is composed of the following parts:

![Sidebar Struture](./images/sidebar-structure.webp)

1. **SidebarProvider**: Handles collapsible state.
2. **Sidebar**: The sidebar container.
3. **SidebarHeader and SidebarFooter**: Sticky at the top and bottom of the sidebar.
4. **SidebarContent**: Scrollable content.
5. **SidebarGroup**: Section within the SidebarContent.
6. **SidebarTrigger**: Trigger for the Sidebar.
